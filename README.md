## Problem

Millions of households rely on firewood and charcoal for cooking, exposing families—especially women and children—to harmful indoor air pollution. In parallel, poor waste management leads to environmental contamination, increasing the spread of disease and negatively impacting community health.


## Solution

Anegon GreenTech Ltd is developing a decentralized waste-to-energy system that converts organic waste—particularly poultry and biodegradable waste into:

- Clean cooking gas (biogas)  
- Renewable energy for productive use  
- Organic fertilizer  

This solution reduces indoor air pollution, improves sanitation, and creates healthier living conditions for children and vulnerable communities.


## How It Works

1. Organic waste is collected from households, farms, and facilities  
2. Waste is fed into a biodigester system  
3. Anaerobic digestion produces biogas  
4. Gas is stored and distributed for cooking or energy use  
5. Residual slurry is processed into organic fertilizer
