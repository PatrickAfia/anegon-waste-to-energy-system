# Anegon GreenTech – Decentralized Waste-to-Energy System

![Anegon Logo](https://imgur.com/Fe1nxJ6)

## Overview

Anegon GreenTech is developing a decentralized waste-to-energy system that converts organic waste into clean biogas and organic fertilizer to improve children’s health, sanitation, and energy access in underserved communities.

Our solution addresses the dual challenge of:
- Indoor air pollution from firewood and charcoal
- Poor sanitation and unmanaged organic waste

By transforming waste into clean energy and agricultural inputs, we create a scalable circular economy model that improves health outcomes, environmental sustainability, and livelihoods.

## Problem

In many low-income communities:

- Households rely on firewood and charcoal for cooking  
- Indoor air pollution contributes to respiratory diseases in children  
- Organic waste is poorly managed, leading to contamination and disease exposure  
- Access to clean, affordable energy is limited  

These challenges directly impact children's health, safety, and long-term development.



## Solution

Our system converts organic waste (poultry, household, and biodegradable materials) into:

-  Clean biogas for cooking and energy  
-  Organic fertilizer (bio-slurry) for agriculture  

The solution is deployed through:

- Household and community-based biodigesters  
- Integrated poultry-based feedstock systems  
- Training of local technicians for installation and maintenance  
- A biogas research and demonstration center for scaling and optimization  

Anegon manages the full value chain—from installation to operation, training, and scaling.


## How It Works

1. Organic waste is collected (poultry, household, agricultural)
2. Waste is fed into anaerobic digesters
3. Biogas is produced and used for clean cooking or energy
4. Bio-slurry is extracted and used as organic fertilizer

This creates a **closed-loop system** that reduces pollution while generating value.
